# Executive Dashboard Story

# Executive Summary

The dashboard provides an overview of retail business performance by combining key performance indicators with sales trends, regional performance, category profitability, shipping performance, and geographic sales distribution. Overall, the business has generated **217,017,652** in total sales and **33,306,313** in total profit, resulting in a **15.35% profit margin**. While overall performance is strong, the dashboard highlights differences across regions, product categories, and shipping methods that leadership should address.

---

# What Is Performing Well

The business is generating healthy overall sales and profit, indicating strong market performance. Technology products contribute the highest profit among all categories, making them an important driver of business success. Regional analysis also shows that some regions consistently generate higher sales and profits, while the state-level map indicates that revenue is well distributed across multiple locations rather than relying on a single market.

---

# What Is Underperforming

Not all product categories contribute equally to profitability. Furniture and some office supply sub-categories generate lower profits compared with technology products. Shipping performance also varies across shipping modes, suggesting opportunities to improve delivery efficiency and customer experience.

---

# What Risks Are Visible

The dashboard indicates that business performance is uneven across categories and regions. Lower-performing categories and shipping delays may reduce customer satisfaction and overall profitability. The current dashboard also highlights that relying heavily on a small number of high-performing categories could increase business risk if market demand changes.

---

# What Opportunities Are Visible

The strongest opportunity is to expand successful product categories and improve sales in lower-performing categories. High-performing regions can be used as benchmarks for other markets. Improving shipping efficiency and monitoring geographic performance can further increase customer satisfaction and support revenue growth.

---

# Recommended Business Actions

- Increase investment in high-performing product categories, particularly Technology.
- Review pricing and promotional strategies for lower-performing categories.
- Improve shipping processes for delivery modes with longer delays.
- Replicate successful practices from high-performing regions across other locations.
- Continue monitoring regional and state-level sales to identify new growth opportunities.

---

# Limitations of the Dashboard

The dashboard presents historical business performance and does not explain the underlying causes of changes in sales or profit. External factors such as market competition, seasonality, customer preferences, and economic conditions are not included. The dashboard should therefore be used alongside other business information when making strategic decisions.

---

# Suggested Next Analysis

Future analysis should include customer segmentation, product-level profitability, sales forecasting, seasonal demand analysis, return behaviour, and customer lifetime value. Predictive analytics could also help estimate future sales and identify potential business risks before they occur.
